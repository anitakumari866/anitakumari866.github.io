// CORE CAPABILITIES
Digital Forensics
Specializations

Our trained officers employ cutting-edge forensic tools and methodologies to investigate, recover, and preserve digital evidence for legal proceedings.

01 / COMPUTE

Computer Forensics

Full disk imaging, deleted file recovery, registry analysis, and metadata extraction from Windows, Linux, and macOS systems.

02 / MOBILE

Mobile Device Analysis

Extraction of SMS, call records, app data, geolocation history, and encrypted messaging from Android and iOS devices.

03 / NETWORK

Network Forensics

Traffic capture and analysis, intrusion detection, IP tracing, dark-web investigation, and ISP coordination for cybercrime cases.

04 / MALWARE

Malware & Ransomware

Malware reverse engineering, ransomware decryption, threat actor attribution, and incident containment for public and private entities.

05 / MEDIA

Digital Media Analysis

Image/video authentication, deepfake detection, metadata forensics, and audio enhancement for evidentiary purposes.

06 / SOCIAL

Social Media & OSINT

Open-source intelligence gathering, fake profile investigation, online harassment tracking, and social media evidence preservation.

// INVESTIGATION PROTOCOL
Forensic Investigation
Methodology

Every digital investigation follows a court-admissible protocol ensuring evidence integrity and chain of custody.

01

Incident Receipt & Triage

Complaint registered, severity assessed, and initial digital footprint mapped. Emergency preservation notices issued if necessary.

02

Scene Securing & Identification

Digital crime scene secured. All devices identified, photographed, documented, and prevented from data alteration or remote wipe.

03

Evidence Collection & Imaging

Bit-for-bit forensic images created using write-blockers. Hash values recorded for integrity verification throughout the process.

04

Analysis & Examination

Deep analysis of images using certified tools. Deleted data recovery, timeline reconstruction, and artifact correlation performed.

05

Reporting & Court Testimony

Comprehensive forensic report prepared per legal standards. Officers provide expert testimony and evidence presentation in court.
